Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of cleaning services tailored to meet the unique needs of various commercial establishments. Our services include:

General Janitorial Services

Our number one service is General Janitorial Services. We are committed to maintaining a spotless and hygienic environment for your facility with our comprehensive cleaning solutions. Our dedicated team performs regular cleaning tasks, including dust mopping, wet mopping, carpet cleaning, vacuuming, surface cleaning, and trash removal. We also clean restrooms, breakrooms, and offices to ensure every part of your facility is attended to. Unlike other services, we do not offer a one-time deep clean, because we are always deep cleaning certain areas with each visit. Our continuous cleaning approach ensures your facility remains in pristine condition at all times. Additionally, we use eco-friendly products and advanced cleaning techniques to provide a safe and healthy environment for everyone in your facility.

Carpet Cleaning

Our professional carpet cleaning services remove dirt, stains, and allergens, leaving your carpets looking and feeling fresh. We use industry-leading equipment and eco-friendly products to deliver exceptional results.

Floor Stripping and Waxing

We provide expert floor stripping and waxing services to restore the shine and protect your floors. Our meticulous process ensures your floors are free of old wax, dirt, and debris before applying a new, durable wax coating.

Specialized Cleaning for Schools and Daycares

We understand the unique cleaning needs of educational facilities. Our specialized cleaning services for schools and daycares focus on creating a safe and healthy environment for students and staff. We pay special attention to high-touch areas and use child-safe cleaning products.
